WHAT YOU GET WHEN YOU LIST

Listing & Scheduling
Create your listing with photos, description, amenities, and pricing. Set your own hourly and daily rates. Define weekly availability down to the hour. Add buffer time between bookings. Choose your cancellation policy. Target specific professions with Professional Use Certifications.
Calendar Sync
Connect Google Calendar to automatically block busy times. Import iCal feeds from Peerspace, Airbnb, or any other platform. Export your Align calendar so other platforms see your bookings. Syncs automatically every 15 minutes.
Booking Management
Accept or decline requests. Approve reschedules. Track check-ins, check-outs, and overtime automatically. Handle no-shows with structured records.
Recurring Bookings
Set up weekly recurring bookings with any guest. Offer a recurring discount (up to 50%) to build loyalty. Control when the discount starts. Pause or cancel anytime.
Arrival Guide
Create step-by-step visual directions with photos. Share WiFi, door codes, and gate codes securely. Automatically emailed to guests the morning of their booking.
Messaging
Direct messaging with guests about specific bookings. Image attachments, read receipts, and push and email notifications so you never miss a message.
Earnings & Referrals
Track total earnings, monthly income, and full payout history. Generate unique referral links for each listing. See click counts, bookings, and conversions per link. Payouts deposited directly to your bank via Stripe.
Reviews & Badges
Guests leave reviews after completed bookings and you can respond directly. Earn badges like Superhost, Top Rated, and Responsive that display on your listing to build credibility.
Protection & Trust
Every guest accepts a mutual agreement for every booking. Verified host insurance. Structured damage reporting with photo documentation and a 72-hour guest response window.
Free Professional Photography
Don't have great photos of your space? No problem. We'll come to you and photograph your workspace at no cost, so your listing looks its best from day one. The photos are yours to use on Align.
